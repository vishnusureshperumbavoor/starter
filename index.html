Welcome to Trichromatic

TRICHROMATIC is a social enterprise working with formal waste pickers and bulk waste generators to create inclusive, financially viable and environmentally sustainable waste management models. Simply put, we help you divert waste from the landfill to green end-points. We are an independent waste management service, and we can offer you professional solutions at an exceptionally cost-effective rate. When you work with us, we can save you a minimum of 20% on the cost of your current waste management services.

We are the licensed [LI No.A3-423/2022/2022-23/257] and registered [Reg. No.KL-02-0036109] service provider in Kerala having registered office at Perumbavoor. TRICHROMATIC is led by Professional Social Worker having wide range of knowledge and experiences in solid waste mangement sector. Trichromatic is servicing 3500+ homes daily. We believe in the concept of "Waste is not Waste, Untill We Waste it", for that, we bring together nature, people and technology to provide the most impactful waste management solutions that maximize resource recovery.

RRR Principles

We strongly believe in the RRR principles of Reduce, Reuse & Recycle concept. We have develped an eco-composting system to reduce the biodegrable waste. Working to meet the needs of all our clients on an individual basis, we offer tailored sevices to assist with the management, storage, collection and disposal of waste. We waste with clients across a range of sectors to help then deal with all types of waste. We have a large space of Material Recovery Facility Centre for Recuse & Recycle of non-biodegradable items. Through RRR principles, we create a Zero waste revolution for descendants.
